Android Survey Applications

Field-ready Android apps for data collection, market research, and audits — offline-first with GPS tagging, photo capture, digital signatures, and instant cloud sync when connectivity returns.

App Features

Survey App Capabilities

Designed for field agents, researchers, and auditors who need reliable data collection in any environment.

Offline-First Data Collection

All form responses, photos, and GPS coordinates stored locally on the device — works without internet and syncs automatically when back online.

GPS Geotagging

Auto-attach precise GPS coordinates and address to every submission — view field agent locations and survey coverage on a live map dashboard.

Photo & Video Capture

Capture and attach photos, videos, barcodes, and QR codes directly within survey questions — compressed and timestamped automatically.

Dynamic Form Builder

Admin web portal to create complex forms — branching logic, skip patterns, validation rules, Likert scales, and multilingual support, all without code.

Agent & Team Management

Assign surveys to individual agents or teams, set quotas, track completion rates, and monitor real-time field activity from the supervisor dashboard.

Analytics & Export

Real-time response dashboards, cross-tabulation, and one-click export to Excel, CSV, SPSS, or API push to your data warehouse.

Our Process

From Form Design to Live Field

Fast deployment — most survey apps go live within 3–4 weeks from requirements sign-off.

1
Requirement Scoping

Define data points, form flow, offline requirements, and integrations — produce a detailed functional specification and app wireframes.

2
App & Portal Build

Android app + web admin portal developed in parallel — agile sprints with test APKs distributed after each cycle for feedback.

3
Field Pilot

Deploy with a small team of agents in real conditions — test offline behaviour, GPS accuracy, sync performance, and form usability.

4
Full Rollout & Training

Scale to full agent team, Play Store (or enterprise) deployment, admin training, and field agent onboarding support.
