LMS & Educational Portals

Custom learning platforms built for training academies, corporate L&D teams, and universities — self-paced courses, live sessions, certifications, and deep learner analytics in one system.

Platform Features

LMS Capabilities

Everything your learners and administrators need — in a platform branded for your organisation.

Course Authoring & Management

Rich course builder with video, PDFs, SCORM, quizzes, and assignments — drag-and-drop authoring for instructors with no technical background.

Live Classes & Webinars

Integrated live session scheduling with Zoom or custom WebRTC — attendance tracking, session recording, and playback for enrolled learners.

Assessments & Certifications

Configurable quizzes, proctored exams, and auto-generated branded certificates on completion — shareable via LinkedIn and verifiable QR code.

Learner Analytics Dashboard

Track enrolment, completion rates, quiz scores, time-on-course, and engagement heatmaps — actionable data for instructors and administrators.

Multi-Role User Management

Admin, instructor, student, and corporate client roles with granular permissions — bulk enrolment, batch management, and SSO integration.

E-Commerce & Subscriptions

Built-in course marketplace with Razorpay / Stripe integration, subscription plans, discount codes, and automated tax invoicing.
