Mobile Applications

Native and cross-platform mobile experiences — Android & iOS apps engineered for seamless performance, intuitive design, and lasting user engagement.

What We Build

Mobile Expertise

From consumer apps to mission-critical enterprise tools — we deliver mobile solutions that users love and businesses depend on.

Android Development

Native Android apps built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose — Material Design 3, offline support, push notifications, and Play Store deployment.

iOS Development

Native iOS apps with Swift and SwiftUI — polished UI, CoreData persistence, Apple Pay integration, and App Store submission support.

Cross-Platform (React Native / Flutter)

One codebase, two platforms. React Native and Flutter deliver near-native performance at a fraction of the cost — ideal for startups and MVPs.

Backend & API Development

Robust RESTful APIs and real-time backends (Node.js, Firebase, AWS) to power your app's data, authentication, and business logic.

Offline-First Architecture

Apps that work without internet connectivity — local data storage, background sync, and conflict resolution for field-use scenarios.

Analytics & Crash Monitoring

Integrated Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, and custom dashboards so you always know how your app performs in the real world.

Our Process

From Idea to App Store

A proven delivery process that gets your app live fast — without cutting corners.

1
Discovery & UX Design

User research, competitor analysis, wireframes, and clickable prototypes — validated with real users before we write code.

2
Development Sprints

Two-week agile sprints with working builds shared after every cycle. Full transparency via TestFlight / internal tracks.

3
QA & Performance Testing

Automated tests, manual QA, device fragmentation testing, and performance profiling before any release candidate.

4
Store Submission & Launch

We handle Play Store and App Store submission, ASO optimisation, and provide post-launch monitoring for 90 days.
