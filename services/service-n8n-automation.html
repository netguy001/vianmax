n8n Workflow Automation

Stop doing repetitive tasks manually. We design and deploy n8n automation workflows that connect your tools, trigger actions automatically, and free your team to focus on work that matters.

What We Automate

Automation Use Cases

n8n connects 400+ apps and services. We build workflows tailored to your exact business processes.

Lead & CRM Automation

Capture leads from any source, enrich data, assign to reps, trigger follow-up sequences, and update CRM records — all without touching a keyboard.

Document & Report Generation

Auto-generate invoices, reports, and PDFs from spreadsheet or database data, then deliver them via email or WhatsApp on schedule.

Data Sync & ETL

Keep databases, spreadsheets, and SaaS tools in sync in real time — pull from APIs, transform, and push to any destination automatically.

Notification & Alerting Pipelines

Set up intelligent alert systems — send Slack, Telegram, or SMS notifications based on business events, thresholds, or schedule triggers.

E-Commerce Order Automation

Automate order confirmations, shipping label creation, inventory deductions, and post-purchase review requests across Shopify, WooCommerce, and custom stores.

Custom n8n Nodes & Integrations

No native integration? We build custom n8n nodes for your internal APIs, legacy systems, or niche SaaS tools — extending automation to every corner of your stack.

Our Process

How We Automate

We map, design, test, and deploy workflows with business outcome in mind — not just technical connectivity.

1
Process Audit

Map out your manual workflows, identify automation opportunities, and calculate potential time savings per month.

2
Workflow Design

Visual flowchart of each automation, approved by your team before any n8n canvas work begins.

3
Build & Test

Develop workflows in a staging n8n instance, run with real test data, and validate all edge cases and error handling.

4
Deploy & Hand Over

Production deployment (self-hosted or n8n Cloud), documentation, and team training so you can manage and extend workflows independently.
