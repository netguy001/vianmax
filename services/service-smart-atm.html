Smart ATM Software

Next-generation ATM management systems — real-time monitoring, intelligent cash forecasting, predictive maintenance, and seamless multi-vendor integration for banks and ATM deployers.

Platform Features

Smart ATM Capabilities

Purpose-built software to maximise ATM uptime, reduce cash idle time, and deliver actionable network intelligence.

Real-Time Network Monitoring

Centralised dashboard for live ATM status — online/offline, cash levels, hardware errors, and transaction throughput across your entire network.

Cash Demand Forecasting

ML-driven cash replenishment forecasting using transaction history and location patterns — reduce over-cassetting and last-cash-out incidents by up to 40%.

Predictive Maintenance

Analyse hardware sensor data to predict card reader, dispenser, and printer failures before they happen — schedule maintenance proactively, not reactively.

Fraud Detection & Alerts

Anomaly detection for skimming, tap transactions, and suspicious withdrawal patterns — real-time SMS/email alerts to security operations teams.

Remote Configuration & Updates

Push software updates, screen content, and configuration changes to any subset of ATMs simultaneously — no truck roll required.

Analytics & Reporting

Transaction trends, uptime SLAs, cash utilisation reports, and device performance scorecards — all exportable and scheduled for stakeholders.
